Attention, validation, or favors - never just to talk. You're her emotional ATM, not her romantic interest.

"Hey, can you help me move?" "I'm having drama with my friends..." "Can you pick me up?" But radio silence when she doesn't need anything.

Your time doesn't matter to her. She treats your schedule like it's completely flexible and disposable.

Intentionally vague about her feelings. She wants the benefits of your attention without giving you clarity or commitment.

Hot and Cold Behavior That Never Stabilizes

Intense one day, distant the next. This emotional rollercoaster keeps you addicted to the highs while accepting the lows.

What This Looks Like:

Amazing date followed by days of silence. Deep conversations then treating you like a stranger. Making you feel special, then making you feel invisible.
